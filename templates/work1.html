001 : LearnNow

LearnNow is a self study app which lets you self study at any place, anywhere or anytime at your convenience. It is basically an app for students in nursery, primary and secondary schools (even useful for students in universities and polytechnics). This app is truly a tool for discovering unique learning skills and has a blend of different contents as far as education is concerned.
